SOUTHPORT FAMILY DAY TRIP

price - £25 / £15

    



Loyal Mizpah presents the Annual Summer` Family Day Trip to the sunny seaside city of Southport. The city makes the ideal escape from the hustle and bustle of the big city. It has over 22 miles of coastline boasting a number of beautiful beaches and stunning natural beauty and has one of the oldest piers in the UK, stretching proudly across Southport beach, when you arrive your day will be pack with fun activities for the whole family. You can go to the theme park, enjoy a game of dominoes or indulge in a bit of retail therapy, why not try have afternoon tea at one of the many seaside restaurants. or If you prefer something more up market you can go to one of the many seaside bars for a glass of Wine, Prosecco or Champagne. Then join us for the afternoon party with Dean Alexander, Mr Smooth and guest . it’s a day you won’t forget. More info call 07958 795 902
